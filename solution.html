What we can do.

• Utilize funds from the CDC Preventive Health and Health Services Block Grant (PHHSBG) to sponsor pop-up clinics to provide no-cost preventive services to those in need, potentially lowering cost of chronic disease treatment

Pop-Up Clinics.

• Ease of access

• Convenient locations

• Evening and weekend hours

• Pop-ups are shown to provide an entry point to ongoing primary care services by offering positive relationships and referrals to ongoing healthcare services

• Low operational cost

• Staffed by volunteers from Florida’s Medical Reserve Corps network

• Builds ongoing relationships with trusted community partners
